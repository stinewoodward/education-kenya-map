Gender Equity in Kenyan Schools

This map shows enrollment in Kenyan schools in 2014 for children in grades 1 through 8. Enrollment is displayed separately for girls and boys. The enrollment figures are aggregated at the county level.

The data come from the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics.
